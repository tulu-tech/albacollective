By using our website or submitting information to us, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

01

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected through our website, forms, questionnaires, applications, intake flows, communications connected to prospective or existing business relationships, analytics and cookies, and certain third-party tools we use to operate, secure, measure, and improve our business.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, tools, or services that we do not control, even if they are linked on our website.

02

Who We Are

Alba Collective is a founder-led strategic consulting business focused on revenue architecture, sales and marketing strategy, conversion improvement, CRM and automation systems, website and UX advisory, sales enablement, customer experience, and related consulting and implementation services.

Because our model includes business analysis, discovery, intake evaluation, and direct work with decision-makers, we may collect both personal information and business-related information in connection with inquiries and client discussions.
