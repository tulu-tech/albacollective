Pattern Recognition

different on the surface.

01

Leads come in.
Nothing converts.

Traffic exists. Enquiries arrive. But something between first touch and decision is breaking trust before it forms.

The system does not.

People are working hard. Without shared process and pipeline discipline, effort becomes invisible noise.

Sales is disconnected.

Budget is spent. Campaigns run. The handoff between marketing and sales is where performance dies quietly.
